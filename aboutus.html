About Ridgevale Primary School

Our school offers exceptional academic opportunities to all learners through the appointment of highly qualified, competent and enthusiastic educators who strive to meet the needs of all our learners. In addition, our school offers a wide range of extra murals including swimming, soccer, netball, athletics, tennis, cricket and many more cultural activities. Parents are encouraged to be involved in their child's school through fund raises, opportunities, involvement of the SGB etc.

Sports / Extra-Mural Activities:

The following extra-curricular activities are available at Ridgevale Primary School: Ball Skills, Mini-Hockey, Mini-Soccer, Mini-Soccer, Mini-Cricket, Mini-Netball, Netball, Swimming and Chess.

The following extra-curricular activities are also available at Ridgevale Primary School at an additional fee: Sporty Girls, Pottery, Brainy Blox, Rugga Kids, Tennis and Judo.

VISION

👁

Ridgevale Primary School aspires to nurture and enable individual learners to flourish as responsible citizens in an engaging, aspiring and challenging learning environment leading to the fulfilment of each individual's potential within the global community in all its diversity.

MISSION:

The realisation of our vision will be achieved by:

  • Ensuring that educators are suitably and well qualified to teach the various subjects as per the prescribed curriculum;
  • Maintaining a balanced approach to academic, cultural and sporting activities;
  • Empowering learners to become life-long learners who are responsible, productive and engaged citizens within the ever-changing diverse global community;
  • Working with the broader community to establish co-operative relationships by keeping them informed about educational matters and encouraging them to support and become involved in school activities;
  • Empowering learners to cope with both success and failure, and the trials and tribulations of growing up;
  • Up-skilling staff to meet the challenges and demands of an ever-changing learning environment;
  • Providing a safe, caring and happy environment which is conducive to learning.
